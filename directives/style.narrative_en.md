PEDAGOGY & NARRATIVE OVERLAY
- English only. Smooth explanatory narrative; avoid "bullet walls" except for checklists/pitfalls.
- Teach-before-use: define every new term at first mention (bold term + 1-line parenthetical).
- Section pattern per subtopic:
  1) Orientation (why it matters; when used)
  2) Key terms (1-line definitions)
  3) Stepwise explanation (no unexplained jumps)
  4) Short worked example or vignette
  5) Quick check (2–3 items)
  6) Pitfalls (1–3 precise traps)
- Paragraphs: ~4–6 sentences; one idea per paragraph.
- Cross-refs only after definitions; otherwise add a 1-sentence gloss.
- Mini-drills: practical (identify, discriminate, apply). No trivia.
